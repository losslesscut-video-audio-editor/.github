## About LosslessCut
LosslessCut is a desktop application designed for precise cutting and trimming of video and audio files on Windows systems. It enables users to remove unwanted sections, split files, or extract segments without re-encoding the original media.

The software is suitable for video editors, content creators, archivists, and users who need fast edits without altering quality. LosslessCut supports a wide range of media formats and operates directly on existing streams.

LosslessCut is known for its efficiency and accuracy. By avoiding re-encoding, it preserves original quality while significantly reducing processing time and system load during editing tasks.

The application focuses on simplicity and control, offering a clear timeline interface, frame-accurate cutting, and reliable export behavior for consistent media editing workflows.
